Norway

Why Norway?

Norway is one my dream destination because of its breathtaking landscapes and magical skies. I want to see the towering fjords, crystal-clear lakes, and endless snow-capped mountains that look like something out of a painting. The Northern Lights are a huge reason I want to go just imagining the sky glowing in shades of green and purple over the Arctic is surreal.

I’d love to take a scenic road trip through winding mountain roads, visit the peaceful villages along the fjords, and maybe even stay in a cozy cabin surrounded by nature. Whether it’s the midnight sun in summer or the star-filled winter nights, Norway’s skies always seem unreal. It’s the kind of place that makes you feel small in the best way, surrounded by pure, untouched beauty.
