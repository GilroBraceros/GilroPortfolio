Japan

Why Japan?

Japan is one my dream destination because it has everything I love the cities, rich culture, amazing food, and stunning landscapes. I want to walk through Tokyo’s neon-lit streets, soak in Kyoto’s peaceful temples, and see cherry blossoms in full bloom.

The food alone is enough reason to go foods like fresh sushi, ramen, and treats straight from the source. I’d love to ride a bullet train, visit Akihabara for anime and gaming, and just soak in the mix of tradition and innovation. It’s a place that feels both futuristic and deeply rooted in history, and I know it would be an unforgettable experience.
